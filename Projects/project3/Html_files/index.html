About Us

Healing Hearts in
Mandore Since 2016

KSC General & Dental Hospital was founded with a singular mission — to bring world-class healthcare to the doorstep of every resident of Mandore, Jodhpur, at a price everyone can afford.

Our dedicated team of specialists has served thousands of patients across general medicine, dental care, OBGYN, and surgery. We are proudly empaneled under the Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY scheme, ensuring cashless care for eligible families.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've answered the most common ones below.

Do you accept Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) cards?
Yes! KSC General & Dental Hospital is officially empaneled under Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY. You can avail cashless treatment for eligible procedures. Bring your PM-JAY card and a valid ID proof to the reception and our team will guide you through the process.
What are your hospital timings?
We are open Monday to Saturday from 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM, and on Sundays from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM. For emergencies, please call +91 94628 44444 at any time.
Is dental treatment painful at KSC Hospital?
We use the latest technology including digital RCT, laser dentistry, and effective local anaesthesia to ensure a virtually painless experience. Our doctors are specially trained in pain-management techniques to keep you comfortable throughout.
How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment by calling us at +91 94628 44444, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the appointment form on this page. Walk-ins are also welcome during hospital hours.
Do you have parking facilities?
Yes, we have dedicated parking space available for both two-wheelers and four-wheelers at our premises on Lal Sagar Road, Mandore, Jodhpur.
Are digital payment methods accepted?
Absolutely! We accept all major digital payment modes including UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), debit/credit cards, and net banking. Cash is also accepted.
